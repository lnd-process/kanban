24 Practical Kanban Board Examples
How to best set up a Kanban Board may vary depending on the focus of the work.



In this sense, the Kanban Board of teams with different scopes of work will be distinct. Some will be simple, but others will be complex with multiple columns and lanes.



There is also no doubt that the Kanban Board is a tremendous tool for improving workflow efficiency because it makes the entire workflow visible, making it easier to be controlled and monitored.



The attached article (Source: Kanbanize) presents twenty-four practical examples of Kanban boards applied to different contexts.
