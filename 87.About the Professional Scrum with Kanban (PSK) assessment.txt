About the Professional Scrum with Kanban (PSK) assessment
How to take the Scrum.org PSK exam?



Anyone can take the test. There are no prerequisites for registering. You only need to pay the fee.



In what follows, I will present some specifications and instructions on how to take the exam.



Specifications:

Price: $200 USD per attempt

Time: 60 minutes

Number of questions: 45

Type of questions: multiple choice, multiple answers, true/false

Passing score: 85%

Open-book: yes

To take the assessment, go to Scrum.org's PSK page, click on BUY PSK I ASSESSMENT (bottom of the page), and pay the fee.

https://www.scrum.org/professional-scrum-with-kanban-certification

Later, you will receive an e-mail from Scrum dot org with the instructions to take the assessment.
