Kanban's Origins: A Story Worth Knowing
The video below tell the exciting story of the revolution that Toyota caused in the automobile market through the application of Kanban in manufacturing.



This story highlights that the essence of Kanban is simple, having a practical and visual way of seeing a flow as a whole, identifying bottlenecks, and allowing teams to act in a more self-managed manner.



Kanban's focus is to be efficient and continuously add value to the customers.
