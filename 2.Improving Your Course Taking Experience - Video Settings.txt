Improving Your Course Taking Experience: Video Settings
Hey there,



I have people from all around the world taking this course.



Even though I work with companies from all around the world and never had an issue with people understanding my English, depending on your region and your level of English, you might struggle a little bit with my accent.



This should not be a showstopper and has a very easy fix: turn on the video subtitles!



I manually curated the subtitles to guarantee their quality.



Turn them on by following the instructions:



1. Open a video.

2. Look for the Closed Captions icon (CC) at the bottom right of the course player on your browser.

3. Move your cursor to the icon and select the option "English."
